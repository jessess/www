Portugal

Portugal is an amazing country with a wide variety of landscapes and culture. This series of photos are taken within a big loop from Lisbon headed east, then to the south, then around the coast headed west, and lastly, back up to Lisbon.

In these photos, you’ll see the towns / beaches of Portalegre, Castelo de Vide, Évora, Beja, Serpa, Mértola, Unknown town in Spain, Faro, Praia de Falesia, Praia São Rafael, Benagil, Sagres & Cape of São Vicente, Zambujera do Mar, Lagos, Vila Nova + many more.
